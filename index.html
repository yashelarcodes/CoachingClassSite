About Us

Founded in 2010, Prashant's Academy has become a beacon of excellence in Navi Mumbai's education sector. With over a decade of successful results, we cater to students from 8th to 12th standard (CBSE/ICSE), as well as aspirants of JEE, NEET, and CET. Our mission is to inspire, mentor, and support every student to achieve their academic goals and beyond.

  • Weekly Progress Reports Shared with Parents
  • Special Sunday Doubt Clearing and Revision Sessions
  • Digital App Support for Test Practice & Materials
